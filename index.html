About Himaju

Purpose and Function

Purpose

The purpose of HME POLIMDO is to develop creative and productive human resources among students of the Electrical Engineering Department, fostering intellectual and spiritual awareness.
This aims to cultivate a scientific campus community that upholds the norms and values of religious morality and Pancasila.


Function

HME POLIMDO has the following functions:

a. To collect, evaluate, and channel the aspirations of students from the Electrical Engineering Department.
b. To provide recommendations and suggestions to the Department of Electrical Engineering leadership and other parties, as long as these recommendations and suggestions do not contradict the organization's bylaws."
